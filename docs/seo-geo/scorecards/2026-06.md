## What moved & why

This is the baseline, so nothing to diff. Key reads:

- **Traffic is mostly Direct (578 sessions) vs Organic (87).** People who already know SES find it by
  name and convert. The site is **not yet winning new discovery search** — that's the growth lever.
- **The brand ranks #1.8 for "storm electrical solutions"** but ~32 for generic service terms. We own
  our name; we don't yet own our market.
- The two new posts (landlord safety, emergency electrician) are indexed and climbing (pos 28, 23) but
  too young to judge.

## How keywords are chosen

1. Pull GSC Queries.csv (28-day).
2. Favour queries with **impressions but mediocre position (≈ 5–20)** — these are the winnable ones.
3. Bias toward **service + western-suburb** intent (Karl's core market) and **commercial-intent**
   topical queries our blog posts target.
4. Review monthly: promote anything newly ranking, retire anything dead.

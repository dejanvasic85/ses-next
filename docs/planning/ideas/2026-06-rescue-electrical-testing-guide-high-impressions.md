# Rescue the electrical-testing guide (3,540 impressions, page 1.4)

## Problem / Opportunity

`/blog/comprehensive-guide-to-electrical-testing` draws **3,540 impressions in 28 days at position
~14** but converts only **3 clicks**. It is the single largest click opportunity on the entire site —
moving this one page from position 14 onto page 1 would likely outperform any new blog post we could
write this month. The related service page `/services/electrical-testing` draws another 1,742
impressions but sits at position 56 (buried).

## Context

From the June 2026 scorecard. The "electrical testing" keyword cluster has huge demand
(`electrical testing` 676 impr/pos 16, `electrical safety testing` 266 impr/pos 24) and SES already
ranks for it — just on page 2. The blog post was already touched in Plan 06 (title/meta updates) and
Plan 07 (AS/NZS credibility signals), so the content base is solid; this is about pushing an existing
near-page-1 asset over the line and wiring it to its service page.

## Rough Scope

- Refresh the post's `title` and `description` in Sanity to sharpen the click-through hook.
- Expand depth / freshness signals (updated date, an added section answering a high-impression
  sub-query like "electrical safety testing" or "switchboard testing").
- Add strong bidirectional internal links between the guide and `/services/electrical-testing` so the
  service page (pos 56) inherits relevance and the guide gets a topical cluster.
- Ensure the guide and service page aren't competing — assign clear primary intent to each.

## Success Signal

The guide moves from ~pos 14 into the top 10 and its monthly clicks rise from ~3 toward double digits;
`/services/electrical-testing` climbs off page 5 in the next scorecard.

## Open Questions

- Are the guide and the service page cannibalising each other for "electrical testing"? Check which
  page Google currently prefers per query before optimising both for the same term.
